Drone Night

On the 19th of September, 2019, we held a Drone Night where we invited two guest speakers, [person 1] and [person 2] to talk about their final year project. Many of our members attended to hear [person 1] and [person 2] talk about how they were using drones for new and innovative solutions. A CompSoc member, Kevin McAndrew, also talked about his winning Show & Tell project where he made a three-winged drone from scratch. Finally, we took a group picture with our DJI Mavic (?).

WordPress Tutorial

On the 2nd of October and 19th of February, we held WordPress tutorials for our members. Members were shown how to setup WordPress on their Skynet hosted websites. With WordPress installed, they could create a full website with little to no code.

Minecraft

The Computer Society has had events both in person and online this semester. We host a Minecraft server for all of our members to use. Members built their own houses and replicated buildings in UL Campus and the surrounding area.

Team Trees

Our society raised €100 from people on campus who visited our event, we matched that donation to €200. Which in the end allowed 217 trees to be planted all around the world.

Recruitment Drive

CompSoc sets up the network infrastructure for every club and society recruitment drive. With over 5km of cable, 8 network switches and many wifi routers, setting up the recruitment drive is a hard yet rewarding task.
